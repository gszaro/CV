Gregory Szaro is an American studio and touring drummer based out of New York City, NY.

Well-known for his creative versatility and musicianship, Gregory attended Berklee College of Music where he received his Bachelors and Masters Performance degrees. He has studied his proficiency under legendary drummers Mike Mangini, Yoel Paez, Kenwood Dennard, Mark Walker, Kim Plainfield, and Dave Dicenso, among others.

Gregory has grown to be a prominent figure in the Boston and NYC studio recording and live performance scene in the styles of Jazz/Fusion/Big Band, Pop/Rock/Top 40, Metal/Progressive, and Broadway/Musical Theatre.

Since 2016 Gregory has been steadily working with Royal Caribbean International as Orchestra Drummer, recently holding the drummer chair for We Will Rock You: The Musical.
